Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is your counselling really free?

⌄

Yes — completely free. Our counsellors are paid by our partner universities, not by students. You'll never be charged for advice or guidance.

Who qualifies for a scholarship?

⌄

Scholarship availability depends on the university. Some universities offer up to 100% fee scholarships for high-scoring students; others may not have a scholarship scheme. Our counsellors will check your eligibility based on your marks and the specific university you choose.

Do you handle government university admissions?

⌄

Yes. We assist with admissions to Anna University, Annamalai University, Bharathiyar University, Pondicherry University, VTU, RGUHS, Sreenarayanaguru Open University, and more.

Can I apply for distance or online programs?

⌄

Absolutely. We handle regular, distance, and online programs across all our partner universities. Many students opt for distance learning while working — we guide you on the best fit.

What is NIOS and who is it for?

⌄

NIOS (National Institute of Open Schooling) provides 10th and 12th certification for students who couldn't complete school through regular institutions. There's no age bar, and it's recognized by all universities in India. We are an authorized NIOS center.

How long does the admission process take?

⌄

For most programs, the process from first counselling call to confirmed seat takes 3–7 working days. During peak admission season, it can take up to 2 weeks. We move quickly to ensure you don't miss deadlines.

Do you offer support after admission?

⌄

Yes. We provide ongoing WhatsApp support for any college-related issues — fee receipts, portal problems, ID cards, hostel queries, and more. You're not on your own once you join.

What is the 40-day Certificate Course?

⌄

We offer a 40-day Advanced Certificate Course in select subjects — a focused short-term program that provides skill-based certification. Ideal for students looking to upskill quickly alongside their regular studies or work.
